# Mawqi3 / موقعي — Brand Identity

## Logo
The Mawqi3 mark is a single-color geometric location pin with a calculated inner field. Inside the pin, a filled angular Meem structure suggests the Arabic name موقعي without becoming a literal illustration. The standard circular pin dot is replaced by a small square to reference bait stations and inspected field points.

## Colors
Primary:       #0f766e — Teal (trust, precision)
Primary Light: #14b8a6 — Teal 400
Accent:        #0284c7 — Blue (technology, clarity)
Dark:          #020617 — Near-black
Light:         #f8fafc — Off-white

## Typography
Arabic: Tajawal ExtraBold (wordmark), Tajawal Bold (headings), Tajawal Regular (body)
English: Tajawal Bold (headings), Tajawal Medium (body)

## Voice & Tone
Arabic: professional, direct, field-operator-friendly; not corporate.
English: concise, action-oriented, no marketing fluff.

## Logo Usage Rules
- Minimum size: 24px (mark), 80px wide (full)
- Clear space: equal to the height of the Meem mark on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, recolor, or add effects
- On dark backgrounds: use logo-dark.svg (white version)
- On primary color backgrounds: use white version only
